Prehistory

Four heroes have been captured by an evil mage for daring to fight for freedom. The mage’s wolves are ready to devour them, but first, they must make their way to the prisoners. Two of the heroes are trapped in a deep, shadowy pit, while the other two are confined in an adjacent chamber, separated only by a heavy, iron-bound door. Their goal is clear: reunite and find a way to escape the pit before the wolves—or the mage—catch up to them.
